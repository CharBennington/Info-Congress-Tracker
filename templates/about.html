Our Mission

Congress Tracker is a non-partisan organization which seeks facilitate the diffusion of essential information to voters all around the world. We believe civic education to be the single most important factor in building and maintaining healthy democratic systems. Transparency is the standard, not the exception.

We aggregate complex legislative data and present it in an understandable format, making government transparency practical for everyday use. Our goal is to eliminate barriers to civic engagement by providing the tools needed to track bills, monitor voting patterns, and stay informed about the issues that matter most.
